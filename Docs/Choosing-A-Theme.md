# Choosing a theme

> Open [../Playground/live-preview.html](../Playground/live-preview.html) in a browser to see a theme
> applied to a full sample document and switch between themes live.

## The families

| Theme | One-line character | Good for |
|-------|--------------------|----------|
| **Apple Dark Mono** (`apple-dark-mono.css`) | Pure neutral grayscale; Apple HIG type scale; nothing but black, white, and gray | Quiet, restrained reading in the spirit of Apple documentation |
| **Claude Dark** (`claude-dark.css`) | Warm dark calibrated to claude.ai; a single `#d97757` accent kept for links and emphasis | A softer, editorial feel with a touch of warmth |

Shared traits: dark by default, free fonts (Inter + Noto Sans SC + JetBrains Mono), left-aligned body
(ragged-right, the Apple convention), generous whitespace, and typographic values sourced from
authoritative design systems where possible.

## How to choose

1. Open the live preview and switch between themes; watch the same document render under each.
2. Look at: line-spacing comfort, column width, heading hierarchy, and how the accent (if any) feels.
3. Once decided, install it per [Install-VSCode.md](Install-VSCode.md) (VS Code) or
   [Install-Typora.md](Install-Typora.md) (Typora).
